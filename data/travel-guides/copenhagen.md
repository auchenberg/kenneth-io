## Do

- Take a boat tour from Nyhavn. They are actually quite good to see the city
    - Other boat tours: https://www.visitcopenhagen.com/copenhagen/activities/9-ways-conquer-copenhagens-waters

- Go for at swim at La Banchina (in the summer).  https://www.visitcopenhagen.com/copenhagen/planning/la-banchina-gdk1096386

- Bike around and try the bike bridges
    - https://www.visitcopenhagen.com/copenhagen/activities/bike-bridges

- Rent an electric boat at cruise around the channels (summer)
    - https://goboat.dk/en
    - https://www.friendships.dk/en/boat-rental-christianshavn/

- Visit Tivoli for the Christmas decorations

- Walk around tha lakes with a coffee
